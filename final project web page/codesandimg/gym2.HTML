Diferent types of training

Cardio

Cardio training is exercise with the purpose of developing cardiovascular or aerobic fitness. Cardiovascular fitness is a good measure of the heart’s ability to pump oxygen-rich blood to the muscles.

Strength

Calisthenics engages various muscle groups by using body weight as resistance. It helps improve strength, flexibility, and endurance through controlled and repetitive movements. The exercises often involve pushing, pulling, bending, and stretching, promoting overall fitness.

Hypertrophy

It all starts when you lift a weight heavy enough to challenge your muscles. This action causes microscopic tears in the muscle fibers. These micro-injuries trigger a biological reaction where the body initiates repair. These scientific processes build muscle during weightlifting by creating newer, stronger muscle fibers to replace the damaged ones, resulting in muscle growth over time.
